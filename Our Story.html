Our Story

Once upon a time, at SLIIT (Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology), there were some students studying business and computer stuff. Instead of doing the usual job hunt or internships, they had this wild idea to start their own company. But get this – it wasn't a tech thing; it was a kitchen thing!
So, they called it SLIIT Chef's Kitchen. Imagine a bunch of friends who love cooking and business coming together to make yummy food and run a business at the same time. It's like they mixed their love for food with what they were learning in their classes.
They didn't just want to make any old food, though. They wanted to make food that was special, different, and really tasty. And that's exactly what they did. Every dish they cooked up was like a little masterpiece, showing off their skills and passion.
But it wasn't just about the food. It was about showing everyone that you can do amazing things if you dare to dream and work hard. These students proved that you don't need a fancy job to start something awesome – you just need an idea and the guts to go for it.
So, if you're ever hungry for something delicious and want to support a bunch of students who are chasing their dreams, come check out SLIIT Chef's Kitchen. It's where good food meets a great story, and every bite is a taste of success.
