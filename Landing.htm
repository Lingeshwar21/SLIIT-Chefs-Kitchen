Our Menu

Spicy Noodles

A ossed in a fiery blend of spices, our noodles pack a punch with every bite. Accompanied by crunchy vegetables and succulent chicken or tofu, all coated in our signature spicy sauce. Served steaming hot for a flavor-packed experience.

Pakistan Biryani

Aromatic basmati rice infused with fragrant spices, tender chunks of marinated chicken or lamb, and caramelized onions. Slow-cooked to perfection, each spoonful delivers a burst of flavors from the heart of Pakistan. Served with a side of cooling raita for a harmonious balance of heat and richness.

Student Cheese Pizza

A classic cheesy delight perfect for any occasion. Our hand-tossed crust is topped with a generous layer of gooey mozzarella cheese, melted to golden perfection. Simple yet satisfying, it's the ultimate comfort food for any student craving a taste of home.
